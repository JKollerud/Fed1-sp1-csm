Date 16th December - 18th December

Visiting Professor of Aeronautics

Professor Sheila Widnall is making a guest appearance at the CSM this december. With her boundless enthusiasm and vivid storeyelling, Professor Widnall captivates the audience, delighting even the youngest attendees with tales of soaring innovations and the magic of flight.

It is our pleasure to announce that Prof Sheila Widnall from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology will be delivering 3 lectures on the development of aeronautics and where the future lies in this exciting ‘space’.

Don’t miss the chance to hear Professor Sheila Widnall from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology share her expertise in a special three-lecture series on the past, present, and future of aeronautics. To reserve your spot, please contact our museum at (555) 123-4567 or email info@csm.org. Seats are limited, so act quickly to be part of this inspiring exploration of cutting-edge advancements and future possibilities in the field of aeronautics.
